# Manage Balance

The Balance Management application is responsible for activities related to the creation and maintenance of the balances of a customer. Balances may be shared between services for an account. 

Types of balances include:
-  Monetary balances - Prepaid balances
-  Non-monetary balances (e.g. minutes etc.)

Manage Balance function includes Replenishment (top-up, recharge) of prepaid balances from accounts (e.g. credit card or credit balance) or Cash. Balance Replenishment and Refill Management services are exposed for balance replenishment.

Balance Replenishment allows the use of various payments to affect a balance means including vouchers, e-Transaction, Direct debit, Credit card or any other form of payment. The service is used by channels – RPOS, Self Care and MyJio, and other third party external applications.

Refill Management allows the recharge requests including balance replenishment to be applied to the OCS systems.

Balance Inquiry service is exposed to enable querying the prepaid account balance (monetary and non-monetary) for connectivity services.

Balance Consumption service is exposed to receive the events from OCS, related to balance (incurred for recurring charges, one time charge, threshold) for sending customer notifications and perform necessary actions.
