Following is a textual walk-through of the Balance Policy Maintenance:maintainBalancePolicies operation.

1.  The consumer application (e.g. Self Care) invokes maintainBalancePolicies operation of Balance Policy Maintenance service to update the account properties.

2.  Balance Policy Maintenance component performs the basic semantic validations such as compliance of the message with structure including the mandatory / optional elements.

3.  ==If Queued Plan Activation request received for JioAirFiberMU Product (identified by /product/specId exist and /product/specId = 'P10056'),==

    1.  ==Balance Policy Maintenance component invokes [queryPlanOffering](../../catalog/Manage Product Offering/Product Offering Inquiry.md#queryplanoffering) operation of [Product Offering Inquiry](../../catalog/Manage Product Offering/Product Offering Inquiry.md) service to retrieve the list of Plan Specifications for the received Plan Offering.==  
	    ==**Note:** specify /planOffering/planID = </planoffer/planID\>, /filterCriterias[name = 'PLANSPECIFICATION']/value = 'PLANSPECIFICATION'==
	
	2.  ==Balance Policy Maintenance component determines if any Plan Specification exists with serviceType = Z0194: JioAirFiberMU Data (identified by /planOffering/planSpecifications[planSpecServiceType = 'Z0194'] exist)==  
	    1.  ==set lbAFMUPlanWithData = true==

4.  Balance Policy Maintenance component retrieves the transaction routing details (Endpoint URL) for the destination instance of OCS. Refer [Routing – Outbound – Service Component to route](../../appendices/Appendix G.md#routing-outbound-service-component-to-route) for details.

5.  Balance Policy Maintenance component translates the request from CMM (Reliance SID) to the proprietary message model of OCS and invokes chargeableItemCharge operation of ChargeableItemCharging service. OCS updates the account / plan properties.

6.  If OCS invocation is successful (identified by status = 'return') and Order Reference Number exist,

    1.  Balance Policy Maintenance component invokes [activateDigitalService](../../../digital/billing/Manage Digital Service Subscription/Digital Service Activation.md#activatedigitalservice) operation of [Digital Service Activation](../../../digital/billing/Manage Digital Service Subscription/Digital Service Activation.md) service by persisting a message to the messaging queue to activate the corresponding digital service plan offering.

    2.  ==If lbAFMUPlanWithData = true ~~Queued Plan Activation request received for JioAirFiberMU Product (identified by /product/specId exist and /product/specId = 'P10056')~~,==  
		1.	Balance Policy Maintenance component invokes [activatePlan](../../../home/fulfillment/Process Service Order/Network Charging Account Management.md#activateplan) operation of [Network Charging Account Management](../../../home/fulfillment/Process Service Order/Network Charging Account Management.md) service by persisting a message to the messaging queue to submit the notification for Queue to Stack movement of JioAirFiberMU Plan Offering to Network (CHF – BNG via. JioFMS).    

    3.  Balance Policy Maintenance component invokes [logCustomerInteraction](../../inventory/Manage Customer Interaction/Customer Interaction Maintenance.md#logcustomerinteraction) operation of [Customer Interaction Maintenance](../../inventory/Manage Customer Interaction/Customer Interaction Maintenance.md) service by persisting a message to the messaging queue to log a customer interaction.

7.  The response received is translated from proprietary message model of OCS to CMM (Reliance SID) and returned to the invoking component.
