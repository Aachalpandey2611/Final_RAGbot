# Manage EWallet

Manage EWallet is responsible for maintenance of the Distributor’s or Retailer’s wallet while performing the various point of sale (POS) operations such as top-up and recharge for a subscriber and transfer of balance from one wallet to another.

EWallet Management service is exposed with Estel as Service Provider to enable the RPOS to get, reserve and release the balance of Distributor’s / Retailer’s wallet. It will also enable to transfer the balance from one Dealer Wallet to another (i.e. between dealer locations a.k.a Distributor to Retailer).
