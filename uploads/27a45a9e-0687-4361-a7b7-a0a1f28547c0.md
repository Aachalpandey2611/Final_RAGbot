### Interaction Diagram

``` mermaid
sequenceDiagram
  autonumber
  participant SAP_CRM as SAP CRM
  participant DAPI_BRP as DigitalApi (Balance Recipient Publication)

  participant MSG_CFP as Messaging (CFP.NOTIFYRECIPIENT.IN.Queue)

  SAP_CRM->>+DAPI_BRP: notifyRecipient (IDOC)
  DAPI_BRP->>DAPI_BRP: translateSAPIDOC2SID

  and Campaign Fulfillment Platform

	end
  end
```

Following is a textual walk-through of the approach to implement Balance Recipient Publication:notifyRecipient.

1.  SAP CRM publishes an IDOC (ZCRMXIF_BENEFICIARY_IDOC) which in turn invokes notifyRecipient operation of Balance Recipient Publication service upon registration or deregistration of Recipient.

2.  Balance Recipient Publication component translates the message from proprietary message model of SAP to CMM (Reliance SID).

3.  Balance Recipient Publication component routes the message by persisting a copy to the messaging queue for each consumers:
    1.  **EDIF:** For EDIF sync
    2.  **Campaign Fulfillment Platform:** To manage and initiate the Bill Plan Change for registration or deregistration of the Recipient(s).
	    1.  ==If /transferDetails/status does NOT exist,== Balance Recipient Publication component persists the message to the message queue of Campaign Fulfillment Platform.
